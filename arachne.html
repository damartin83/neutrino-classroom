  Arachne is MINERvA's event display. In these exercises, a simple version of Arachne will allow the students to search for and analyze data from the MINERvA detector.

Supported browsers include Chrome, Firefox, Safari, but not Internet Explorer (sorry).

Test Arachne on your computer by looking at stream of consciousness data from MINERvA. Your actual results will vary.
